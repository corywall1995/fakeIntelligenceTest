Fake Intelligence Test

Welcome to my intelligence test. During this test you will be presented with examples in the form x, y -> z (x and y yields z) and an incomplete example. Your job is to figure the puzzle out and input the answer in the text box provided. If you get it correct a message will display to let you know its correct, if you're incorrect a message will be display telling you so. If the questions prove too difficult you may use the hint button. Using a hint will only take off half credit should you get the answer correct after using the hint. Once you use you hint the skip option will become available to you. You may begin the test by clicking here or by clicking on the "start test" link to the right.
